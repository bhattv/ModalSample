More than 40% of HCA hospitals use NDNQI®

NDNQI is the largest database of nursing quality indicators in the country. When you have the right data, you can see where you stand, and where you need to be. Many CNOs at the Hospital Corporation of America (HCA) choose NDNQI to provide a fuller picture of their facilities' nursing quality right down to the unit-level. Leverage our data in the boardroom and the practice setting to support quality improvement measures.
